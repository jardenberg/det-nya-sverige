# Content Mapping: Old 15 Points → New 15 Points

## Core Shift
The new version has a fundamentally different framing: **integration as onboarding, state as platform, data as infrastructure, AI as lever, human as protagonist.** The core rule: "AI where it reduces friction, humans where they exercise power."

## Key Tone Changes
- Much more direct, punchy, almost provocative
- Technology/AI woven into EVERY point, not as separate point
- "Design problem" framing throughout
- Less poetic, more operational
- Shorter sentences, more edge
- References to real Swedish policy (Digg, IMY, Skolverket, EU AI Act)
