# Image Prompts for 15 Points

Style reference: Warm vintage retro illustration, muted earth tones (cream, dusty blue, warm brown, sage green), hand-drawn feel with fine line work, slightly textured paper background, 1950s editorial illustration style. NO text in image. Rectangular 1200x630 format for OG sharing. NOT round.

1. Ett Sverige in: A friendly robot and a human guide standing together at a welcoming gateway/door, with multiple bureaucratic paths merging into one clean road. Swedish government buildings in background.

2. 100-dagarsgaranti: A person holding up a glowing digital passport/document while old paper files dissolve into light. A clock showing speed. Professional tools (stethoscope, wrench, laptop) floating nearby.

3. Svenska genom arbete: A doctor in scrubs speaking two languages (speech bubbles in different scripts), with a phone showing a language learning app. Hospital setting with warm tones.

4. Snabbfil till bristyrken: A highway with a fast lane, workers of different professions (nurse, electrician, engineer) zooming forward while old barriers/gates open up.

5. Första jobbet inom 180 dagar: A bridge being built between two sides - one with waiting people, the other with welcoming employers. AI/digital elements helping construct the bridge.

6. Bosätt efter möjlighet: A large interactive map/dashboard of Sweden with glowing dots showing opportunities, jobs, schools. A family looking at it together, planning their path.

7. Barnen först: A diverse classroom with children, a warm teacher at the center, digital tablets and books coexisting. Multiple language scripts on a chalkboard. Warm, hopeful.

8. Hård mot exploatering: A shield protecting a worker, with data streams exposing hidden bad actors in shadows. The worker holds a phone showing their rights. Justice scales nearby.

9. Nolltolerans mot diskriminering: A hiring scene where CVs are anonymized (faces blurred/equal), a scale perfectly balanced, diverse professionals standing equal. An AI being audited.

10. Digitalt medlemskap: A person arriving in Sweden and immediately receiving a glowing digital ID card/phone, with Swedish digital services lighting up around them like a welcome constellation.

11. Enklaste landet att starta företag: An entrepreneur at a café table with just a phone, launching a business - a small rocket/shop emerging from the screen. Swedish cityscape behind.

12. Student till byggare: A graduation cap transforming into building blocks/tools, with a graduate walking a clear path from university to workplace/lab/startup. No dead ends.

13. Cirkulär rörlighet: A globe with Sweden at center, connected by glowing lines to people around the world. Suitcases with Swedish flags, digital connections, a revolving door.

14. Snabb rättsstat: A person tracking their case on a phone like tracking a package, with a clear timeline/progress bar. Government building in background, transparent and open.

15. Integration som elitgren: A large open dashboard/scoreboard showing Sweden's municipalities, with people of all kinds contributing data. Open APIs represented as building blocks. Celebration.
